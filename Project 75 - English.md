## Project 75 — Complete Setting Archive

---

### I. Worldview: The Rusted Lighthouse

**Historical Setting:**  
The United States of America in 2036 has completely transformed into a "legalized machine of violence." The federal government is tightly controlled, from top to bottom, by the military-industrial complex, the giant pharmaceutical dynasties, and a bureaucratic class of lawyers turned administrators. The national budget is a black hole: 60% flows overseas for subversion and war, 30% disappears through layers of exploitation, and only 10% barely props up the crumbling livelihood system.

**Social Landscape:**  
- **Infrastructure:** Cracked roads, rusted bridges, a power grid as fragile as a spider's web. New technology serves only the powerful; ordinary cities seem frozen in the last century.  
- **Healthcare:** The medical insurance system is the greatest scam. Insurance companies use algorithms to decide life and death; hospitals become profit centers; expensive drugs cannot cure disease, only sustain "paid suffering."  
- **Justice:** The law is a toy of the lawyer class. Acquitting the rich, convicting the poor; parole and sentence reductions are openly priced.  
- **Public Sentiment:** The bottom of society is split into two camps — one numb and fallen, dependent on drugs and cheap entertainment; the other burning with rage. Project 75 is the most extreme crystallization of that rage.

### II. Core Organization: Project 75

**Meaning of the Name:**  
"Project" denotes the systematic, planned nature of its operations — never the venting of a mob. "75" carries multiple meanings: the initial average age of the founding core members (75), a metaphor for the American founding anniversary (which they view as "old code that needs to be corrected"), and the "75% success rate floor" they pursue in their actions.

**Member Codenames and Identities (Initial Five):**  
Each codename maps precisely to the skill of their previous life, endowing it with a new meaning of death:

1. **Barrett** — *Retired military, sniper*  
   - The spiritual leader and strategist of the organization. He believes in "precision strikes" and considers chaos an excuse of the weak. He evaluates the value of targets and devises the most efficient elimination plans.  
2. **Wrench** — *Auto mechanic, demolition expert*  
   - Taciturn, keeping company with machines. He despises the corrupt officials who paralyze public transportation. He excels at turning everyday objects into explosives, subscribing to the destructive aesthetic of "demolish, never shoot."  
3. **Trojan** — *Programmer, hacker*  
   - Once wrote security code for the government; rebelled after his pension was devoured by an algorithm. He is the eyes and ears of the organization, responsible for penetrating systems, falsifying records, erasing tracks, and screening the list of targets with "definitive evidence of guilt."  
4. **Faucet** — *Firefighter, chemistry and toxicology enthusiast*  
   - Spending years at fire and accident scenes, he has seen enough civilians die from shoddy building materials and corner-cutting. He heads the organization's "Chemistry Department," passionately developing odorless, hard-to-detect poisons, practicing the principle of "poison over gun."  
5. **Second Engineer** — *Deep-sea sailor, logistics and escape master*  
   - Having spent half his life on turbulent seas, he is expert in supply coordination and escape route planning. He is in charge of material support and extraction for every operation, and the only member of this suicidal organization who is allowed to "think about how to stay alive."

**Membership Standards:**  
- **Hard Age Requirement:** 65 years or older.  
- **Entry Threshold (must meet one):** Crippling medical debt; a terminal illness (e.g., late-stage cancer, ALS); the loss of all pension due to government corruption; or the death of an immediate family member through medical error / unaffordable drug prices.  
- **Absolute Taboo:** **No lawyers are accepted.** In the organization's view, lawyers are the technical backbone and whitewashers of the corrupt system; their very existence is the legalization of evil.

### III. Action Philosophy: Cold-Blooded Extreme Justice

The organization's creed: "**We are not here to punish evil. We are here to pull out the root of the disease.** " This drives their strategy far beyond ordinary vengeance.

**Core Operating Principles:**  
1. **Evil dies before it speaks:** Absolute silence during operations — no manifesto, no reaction time, no chance to call for help. They believe the "trial" was completed during the pre-action investigation; the operation itself is merely the execution of a sentence.  
2. **Efficiency first:** If a bomb can create an "accident," no gun is drawn. If poison can disguise itself as "natural death," no blood is spilled. One shot through-and-through is never followed by a second. The goal is to make death look like the inevitable result of "systemic failure," not a personal assassination.  
3. **Collateral purge:** This is the cruelest and most controversial point. The organization believes corruption is an ecosystem, not a matter of individuals. The spouse, adult children, and even grandchildren — enjoying their dividends at elite academies — of a pharmaceutical executive are all considered part of the "bloodsucking ecology." Their logic: "**A child's innocence, in the act of sucking the people's marrow, has long been tainted. To sever the root, even the seedlings cannot be spared — otherwise it will grow into a great tree again.** "  
4. **Executioners of cultural heresy (kill-order against indoctrinators):** The organization takes a cold, indifferent stance toward the LXXXX+ community itself. They neither agree nor hate, as long as it remains a purely personal choice — they regard it as "meaningless noise" in a decadent world. However, the moment this identity is combined with the power to "indoctrinate children," the nature changes completely. Teachers, doctors, child psychologists — anyone who uses professional authority to systematically promote or guide LXXXX+ ideology in minors is defined as a "predator of the spiritual domain." The organization considers such behavior a poison deeper than embezzlement, "twisting the unformed roots of a civilization." Therefore, such targets are placed on the highest-priority kill list, and their elimination is even colder than that of corrupt officials: no economic evidence is required — only confirmation of the act of "exerting improper influence on children" is enough to warrant execution. In their eyes, this is not hatred; this is "removing a source of contamination."

### IV. Organizational Situation and Multilateral Games

**1. Government Response (Failed Counter-Infiltration):**  
The FBI and CIA have tried to dispatch elderly retired agents to infiltrate the organization. However, these agents see a group "more like a government than the government itself," with clear objectives, while they themselves have been abandoned by the state through meager pensions and terrible healthcare. This enormous gap caused near-total failure of the counter-infiltration — **the vast majority of agents defected, becoming the organization's "Deep Throats" or double agents**, feeding internal intelligence back and corroding the corrupt system.

**2. Public Opinion (Torn Populace):**  
- **Supporters (about 45%):** Mostly the bottom, the wronged middle class, and veterans. They see Project 75 as the "last Robin Hood," the only valve in a rusted system that can still roar. On the darknet, they supply tips, donate, and even become peripheral scouts.  
- **Opponents (about 35%):** Mostly vested interests, centrists, and violence-fearing civilians. They call it a "terrorist organization" and an "extreme geriatric killer squad," believing that violence only drags society deeper into the abyss.  
- **The Silent (about 20%):** They sympathize in secret but dare not speak. They are the largest, most oppressed backdrop of the story.

### V. Narrative Suggestions

- **Timeline Anchor:** Begin with "the first drop of blood" on election night 2036, then flashback to the five men's extreme-sport era, showing the evolution from "looking to die" to "looking for meaning."  
- **Suspense Setup:** The core mystery could be — what is the organization's ultimate goal? Mere assassinations, or a "big event" big enough to shake the system, detonated at the "Beren government's" birthday banquet?  
- **Humanity Interrogation:** Through the "collateral purge" setting, deeply explore the "boundary of justice." When the protagonists personally kill a ten-year-old child, will their belief hold firm? Whose side will the reader take?
