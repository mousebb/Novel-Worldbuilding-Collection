# 《The Abyssal Mother》World Setting

---

## I. Background: A Migratory Survival from the Deep Sea

Due to global warming and the intensification of El Niño, ocean temperatures shift violently. The deep-sea ecological environment suffers unprecedented damage. An unknown intelligent organism — **"The Mother"** — that has long been dormant in the ocean depths is forced to leave its native deep-sea habitat.

The Mother is not a traditional individual organism, but more like a **superorganism** maintained by a **hive consciousness**. Its main body always lies dormant in the depths of the trench, reproducing and expanding by releasing extremely tiny transparent symbionts. These transparent beings are highly camouflaged in seawater, surfacing only under specific conditions.

The reality of environmental degradation forces the Mother to launch an unprecedented evolutionary plan: **transform its offspring from a deep-sea species into a terrestrial one**, to continue the survival of the species.

---

## II. Reproduction Chain: Three Generations and Two Sources

### Definitions

- **First Generation (G1):** Adult childbearing-age women parasitized by the transparent symbionts. They are the only group that passes through the "infection" link, giving birth to G2 daughters.
- **Second Generation (G2):** Half-human beautiful girls. Divided into naturally born (born of G1 women) and artificially induced (born through commercial activity). G2 women can mate with human men to **directly produce more G2 women**, and after sex change can also pair with another G2 woman to produce G3.
- **Third Generation (G3):** Complete forms. Born of a G2 woman and a sex-changed G2 man. DNA undetectable, special brain structure (invisible under MRI/CT), can receive the Mother's summons.

---

### First Generation: The Vessel — Parasitized Childbearing-Age Women

- **Mode of Infection:** Transparent symbionts invade through mouth, nose, or ears in childbearing-age women active at sea surface (swimming, drowning, shipwreck, etc.). The process is very brief; the victim and society often mistake it for ordinary drowning.
- **Physiological Effects:**
  - Craving for seafood (fish, shrimp, seaweed, etc.).
  - Shortened menstrual cycle, abnormally increased ovulation.
  - Significantly increased libido, actively seeking intercourse with strong men.
  - After conception, only **female infants** are born, all carrying mutated chromosomes.
- **Outcome:** After giving birth to G2, G1 women often suffer progressive physical decline or mental breakdown; the vast majority lose their minds or die within a few years. They are the "tools" of the chain, consumed to exhaustion.

---

### Second Generation: The Newborn — Half-Human Beautiful Girls

**The Second Generation has two fundamentally different origins, determining the divergence of "identity" and possible story trajectories.**

#### Source A: Natural Birth (Within the Mother's Plan)
- Born of parasitized G1 women.
- This is the reproduction path originally designed by the Mother.
- These G2 girls carry the Mother's "mission" from birth, but possess independent human consciousness.

#### Source B: Artificial Induction (Human Commercial Activity)
- **Unscrupulous merchants or criminal organizations** kidnap or lure human women and push them into waters where the Mother is active, causing them to be actively infected by the transparent symbionts.
- After infection, conception, and birth of G2, these women are often directly abandoned or silenced.
- From the beginning, G2 girls from this source are regarded as **commodities** rather than lives; their "factory exit" route is mapped out before birth.

**Biologically, the two sources of G2 are completely identical, but their "identity" is worlds apart:**
- G2 from Source A may have a family (though the G1 mother often dies early), an identity, an initial web of human relationships.
- G2 from Source B are "rootless"; they are born into a black-market environment, possibly trained and traded from a young age, full of distrust or hatred for humans.

#### Common Characteristics of G2 (Regardless of Source)

- **Appearance:** Outwardly fully human, but more refined in looks, with flawless, lustrous skin and supernormal attractiveness.
- **Physiological Characteristics:**
  - Carries the Mother's genes, identifiable by standard DNA tests (unless specially treated? Optional).
  - Possesses a **one-time, irreversible sex-change ability:** under specific triggers, the female reproductive organs can transform into male organs; once changed to male, identity cannot be reverted to female.
    - **Natural trigger:** Remotely activated by the deep-sea Mother via chemical/biological signals, for reproduction needs.
    - **Artificial trigger:** Unscrupulous scientists decipher the trigger mechanism and forcibly activate it through technical means.
  - After sex change, capable of impregnating other G2 women. A pair can produce **G3**.
  - **Precocious:** G2 women reach sexual maturity significantly earlier than human women, usually at ages 12–15. This allows them to be put to commercial use earlier.
  - **Cost of Reproduction:** Each pregnancy accelerates the G2 woman's aging. After 2–3 births, her appearance and physical functions rapidly decline to those of a human woman in her 40s or 50s, losing commercial value.
  - **Senescence beyond optimal age:** Even if never pregnant, once past the optimal reproductive age (about 18–28), G2 women begin to age rapidly, becoming withered within a few years. This means their natural "shelf life" as commodities is limited.

- **Special Rules for Pairing with Human Men:**
  - If a G2 woman mates with an **ordinary human man** and conceives, the offspring is **not a G3** but — **a female infant identical to G2** (carrying the mutant gene, identifiable by DNA test, growing up with all the same G2 potential).
  - This means: **human men are "factories" for making G2.**
  - The closed business loop: unscrupulous merchants buy a G2 girl → have her mate with human men → produce more G2 girls → continue selling or repeat use.

#### Cognition and Will of G2

G2 women possess independent human consciousness and emotion, but harbor instinctual drives of the Mother. Most of them do not know their true identity until triggered or informed. G2 from different sources may hold sharply different attitudes toward "humanity" and "the Mother."

---

### Third Generation: The Complete Form — Undetectable "Antennas"

- **Mode of Birth:** The **sole path** is the natural pairing of a G2 woman with a sex-changed G2 man. Pairing with a human man cannot produce G3.
- **Biological Characteristics:**
  - **Undetectable DNA:** G3's genetic sequence cannot be identified as abnormal by any existing human technology. Any genetic screen will mark them as normal human.
  - **Special Brain Structure:** A micro-structure exists in their brain that humans do not have — equivalent to a "receiving antenna."
  - **Can be summoned by the Mother:** Once the Mother sends a signal, G3 individuals can receive and obey orders. The summons can be a vague "impulse" or a precise command.
  - **Sex:** G3 can be male or female, but no longer possess G2's sex-change ability.
- **Strategic Significance:** G3 is the **main force** in the Mother's terrestrial expansion. They can integrate perfectly into human society; from appearance to genes there is no identifiable anomaly. Once their numbers reach a critical point, they can act collectively under the command of the Mother or a new Mother.

                                │                           │
            ┌───────────────────┼───────────────────┐       │
            │                   │                   │       │
    (Mating with human man)   (Natural sex change)  (Artificial sex change)
            │                   │                   │       │
            ↓                   ↓                   ↓       │
       [G2 woman]      [Sex-changed G2 man]   [Sex-changed G2 man]
       (Loop)                 │                   │
                             └─────────┬─────────┘
                                       ↓
                                 [G2 woman] ──────────────┘
                                       │
                                   (Mating)
                                       ↓
                                 [G3 complete form]
                          (DNA undetectable, brain structure hidden)

**Three key reproduction paths:**

| Path | Combination | Product | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Path 1 | Human woman → infection | G1 woman → G2 | The Mother's natural/original reproduction mode |
| Path 2 | G2 woman + human man | G2 woman (loop) | **Core of the human commercial reproduction chain:** unlimited "production" of G2 |
| Path 3 | G2 woman + sex-changed G2 man | G3 complete form | The Mother's true evolutionary goal: the terrestrial species |

---

### Supplement: The Key Rules of Reproductive Isolation

**Strict reproductive isolation exists between the Mother's descendants and humans.** The reproductive results of different combinations are as follows:

| Combination | Can reproduce? | Product | Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Ordinary human man + ordinary human woman | Yes | Ordinary human | Normal |
| Ordinary human man + G1 woman | Yes | G2 woman | G1 has been parasitized; conception always produces a G2 woman |
| Ordinary human man + G2 woman | Yes | G2 woman | **Core of the commercial loop** |
| Sex-changed G2 man + ordinary human woman | No | Cannot conceive | **Key isolation:** the sperm of a sex-changed G2 man is incompatible with human eggs |
| Sex-changed G2 man + G1 woman | No | Cannot conceive | Maintains consistency: sex-changed G2 men also cannot impregnate parasitized women |
| Sex-changed G2 man + G2 woman | Yes | G3 complete form | **The only path that produces G3** |
| G2 woman + G2 woman (both unaltered) | No | Infertile | Requires one party to change sex |

**Why is this setting important?**
- It ensures the "purity" of the Mother's population: only G2-with-G2 can produce the ultimate offspring (G3).
- It prevents humans from "capturing sex-changed G2 men to breed new species."
- It enhances the preciousness of G3: every pair that can produce G3 must be the combination of "G2 woman + sex-changed G2 man." Neither can be missing.

## IV. "G2 Farms": The Dark Industrialization of Commercial Reproduction

Based on Path 2 (G2 woman + human man = G2 woman), unscrupulous merchants establish **G2 Farms**.

### Business Model

- **Core model:** Purchase or capture G2 women, repeatedly mate them with human men ("studs") to continuously produce new G2 women. Some of the new G2 women stay on the farm as "breeding stock," others are sold.
- **Artificial-induction supplement:** Also kidnap ordinary human women, force them to be infected into G1, and mate with human men to produce G2 women.

### Product Lines and Exploitation

- **Live sales:** G2 women are sold as beautiful pets, sex slaves, luxury items. Buyers must mind their "shelf life" — G2 women who have not given birth will usually age rapidly before age 28, so buyers demand younger products.
- **Forced reproduction:** Some G2 women are used as birthing machines. After 2–3 pregnancies they age and are scrapped, then discarded or destroyed.
- **Hysterectomy:**
  - To extend the shelf life of G2 women as "beauty commodities," unscrupulous merchants **remove the womb of commodity-type G2.** Without a womb, the G2 woman cannot bear children and therefore will not age quickly through pregnancy. At the same time, the trigger mechanism for senescence-beyond-optimal-age is unrelated to whether the womb is retained, but after hysterectomy, merchants can claim she "will not age" (in reality she will still age past the optimal age; she just loses the accelerated factor of pregnancy).
  - Removed wombs have extremely high black-market value:
    - **Transplanted into human women:** Rumors say that human women receiving a G2 womb can temporarily gain G2-like beauty and fertility (but with possible rejection or parasitism risks).
    - **Research use:** Biotech companies attempt to reverse-engineer the Mother's genetic secrets from womb tissue.
    - **Collector items:** The macabre collections of extreme tycoons.
  - Hysterectomy is usually performed when the G2 woman has just entered reproductive age (12–15), to maximize her commercial value. These womb-less G2 women will never bear children, can only exist as pure ornaments/playthings, and will be discarded once they pass the optimal age.

### Social Impact

- The number of missing women in these regions will rise sharply, but local governments and the international community may ignore this for a long time.
- G2 farms may grow into vast transnational criminal networks involving human trafficking, organ trade, illegal experiments, and more.
- Some local corrupt officials, police, and even health-department personnel may be bribed, becoming the industry's protective umbrella.
- Some G2 women attempt to flee or resist after hysterectomy, but with a very low success rate.

### Impact on the Mother's Plan

- **Unintended acceleration:** Human commercial activity is in fact **helping the Mother expand massively.** Originally limited by the natural probability of G1 infection, humans have now broken that limit themselves.
- **Population differentiation:** G2 girls born on farms, treated as commodities from birth, with no human kinship or social ties, may harbor hatred toward humans far beyond that of naturally born G2. When the Mother finally summons, they may become the most loyal, most brutal soldiers.
- **Identity dilemma:** Complex relationships may form between naturally born G2 (with family, with identity) and farm-born G2 (rootless, commodified) — sympathy, contempt, cooperation, conflict.
- Large-scale hysterectomy **seriously disrupts the Mother's reproduction plan**, because it means large numbers of G2 women cannot participate in reproduction (whether producing G2 or G3).
- This may provoke a fierce response from the Mother or a new Mother — for example, forcibly awakening all G3 to cleanse the human womb black market.

## V. Sex Change: One-Way Tooling and Commodification

The sex-change ability is the most central special property of G2 half-blood girls, and also the most tragic and commercially valuable setting in the entire story.

### Trigger Mechanism

- **Natural trigger (Mother's summons):** When the deep-sea Mother judges that the population needs expanding, it remotely selects certain G2 girls to trigger the sex change. The process is irreversible — once complete, the individual permanently becomes male.
- **Artificial trigger (human simulation):** By reverse-engineering the Mother's trigger signal, human scientists can manufacture a simulated trigger device. Mastering this technology means:
  - **A G2 girl can be turned into a man at will**, used to breed G3, or for other purposes.
  - **The technology can be abused** for human trafficking, forced reproduction, human experimentation.
  - **Possible counter-use:** For example, forcing mass trigger of G2 sex change, depleting the Mother's reproductive reserves, or inducing genetic collapse.

### The Tragedy of Sex Change

- **One-time identity deprivation:** Sex change is not a "superpower" you can flip back and forth, but a one-way street. Once a G2 girl is triggered into a man, she permanently loses her former body and identity.
- **The extreme of tooling:** Their bodies do not belong to them — they belong to the Mother, the market, the powerful. Their "value" comes precisely from what they can lose.

### Linkage Between Sex Change and Commercialization

In the context of "G2 farms" and "commodification," sex-change ability gains new commercial uses:

- **G3 production line:** Unscrupulous merchants can buy two G2 girls, trigger one into a male, then force the pair to mate, producing **G3 complete forms.** G3's DNA is undetectable, meaning they can become perfect **spies, assassins, smuggling vectors** — far more valuable than G2.
- **Sex-change performances:** In some extreme underground entertainment venues, G2 girls are forcibly triggered to change sex, for rich audiences to watch — a beautiful girl painfully turning into a man on stage. This is one of the cruelest forms of exploitation.

### Reproductive Limits of Sex-Changed G2 Men

- Once a G2 girl is triggered into a man, he **cannot impregnate any "pure human" woman** (including ordinary human women and G1 women).
- This completely blocks the path of humans using sex-changed G2 men as "studs" to breed more offspring.
- The only reproductive value of a sex-changed G2 man is to pair with **another G2 woman** to produce a G3 complete form.
- This limit also means that if a sex-changed G2 man tries to start a family with a human woman, he cannot have his own offspring — a profound tragedy.

## VI. The New Mother

When the terrestrial G2 and G3 populations reach a certain critical size, a deeper question emerges: **will the deep-sea Mother produce a new "Queen"?**

### Natural Swarming — The New Queen of the Terrestrial Population

When the terrestrial G2 and G3 populations reach a critical size (e.g., the total surpasses a threshold, or the Mother detects the original Mother's life fading), a natural mechanism activates:

- A special **G3 individual** (usually female) begins to evolve into a "new Mother."
- The new Mother has the following abilities:
  - Can send her own summoning signals to surrounding G2 and G3, establishing an **independent hive network.**
  - If the original Mother dies, she can take over as the new core of the species.
  - Different new Mothers may cooperate, compete, or even fight "Queen wars."
- **Consequence:** Humanity will face the rise of one or more new types of intelligent species. They will no longer be simple "monsters" but **competitors** with organization, strategy, and even civilization.

### A Desperate Move — Humans Build an Artificial Mother

After discovering the Mother's existence and the undetectable nature of G3, humanity (especially major military departments or large biotech companies) will attempt an extreme response:

- By capturing G2 and G3 individuals, combined with gene editing and neuroscience, **create a "controllable new Mother" artificially.**
- Goal: Use this artificial Mother to fight the original Mother, seize control of G2 and G3, or as the ultimate weapon.
- **Risks:**
  - The artificial Mother may lose control and become a more terrifying existence than the original.
  - This will trigger an ethical nuclear bomb: do humans have the right to create a new intelligent species as slaves and weapons?
  - The original Mother may view the artificial Mother as **a violation of reproductive rights**, and thus launch a total offensive in advance.

**Two threads can interweave:** In the process of creating an artificial Mother, humans accidentally accelerate the birth of a natural new Mother; or, after awakening, the natural new Mother actively competes/cooperates with the artificial one.

## VII. Starting Point: Coastal Impoverished Regions of South America and Africa

### Reasons for Site Selection

The Mother's invasion plan does not begin synchronously across the globe. The first stage focuses on **coastal impoverished regions of South America and Africa**, because these areas provide the perfect initial breeding ground:

- **Poor medical conditions:** Drowning, abnormal menstruation, sexual-behavior changes are not recorded or investigated.
- **Fragile social structure:** Severe gender inequality; women's bodies are long-objectified and dominated; accusations of "promiscuity" easily mask biological anomalies.
- **High population mobility:** G2 girls, as laborers, brides, and migrants, flow into developed countries, scattering the seed silently across the globe.
- **Regulatory blind spots:** International health organizations and governments lack sustained attention to health data in these areas.
- **Hotbed of black industries:** These regions are also major hubs of human trafficking, illegal labor, and organ trade — providing a ready-made network and cover for "G2 farms."

### Examples of Specific Regions

- **South America:** Coastal slums of northeastern Brazil, Pacific-coast fishing villages of Peru and Ecuador, Caribbean coast of Colombia.
- **Africa:** West Africa (Gulf of Guinea coast — Nigeria, Ghana, Côte d'Ivoire), East Africa (Mozambique, Tanzania coast), Atlantic coasts of Angola and Congo (Brazzaville).

These regions share common features: tropical coastlines, dense impoverished populations, fishing as primary livelihood, very low medical coverage, severe gender power imbalance, active black markets.

## VIII. Human Society's Response: Greed Is More Frightening Than Invasion

When a silent race-replacement scheme hidden in poor regions is gradually uncovered, humanity's first reaction is not fear and unity, but — **greed.**

### G2 Girls Become Commodities

- **Commodification of beauty:** The beauty of G2 girls is a priceless luxury. On the black market, "G2 girlfriend rentals," "G2 beauty pageants," and "G2 private collections" will emerge.
- **Commercial value of sex-change ability:**
  - G3 production line: A pair of G2 can produce DNA-undetectable G3 complete forms, used for espionage, assassination, smuggling.
  - Reproduction industry: G2 + human man can loop unlimitedly to produce new G2, forming a "perpetual-motion factory."
  - Pornographic entertainment: "Sex-change nightclubs" — watching G2 girls change sex instantly on stage.
  - Medical research: Cracking the sex-change mechanism could revolutionize gender reassignment surgery.
- **Competition among nations and capital:** Biotech companies, gangs, and even government agencies in developed countries will flood into South American and African coasts, "procuring" and "hunting" G2 girls like the colonizers of history.

### The Existence of G2 Farms

Once unscrupulous merchants discover the rule "G2 + human man = new G2," **G2 farms** emerge.

These farms are often hidden in remote coastal areas, guarded by armed personnel. Human women are kidnapped and sent in, forcibly infected, forcibly impregnated. The G2 offspring are sorted: "qualified" ones are sold, "defective" ones may be destroyed or used for other purposes.

Some G2 women stay on the farm as "breeding stock," repeatedly mating with human men, continuously producing new G2. The latter either are sold or, after growing up, become new G2 used for production.

### The Social Collapse Triggered by G3

Once humans discover the existence of **completely undetectable G3 lurkers**, society falls into a chain of suspicion:

- You cannot know whether your neighbor, colleague, spouse, or child is a G3.
- Panic may evolve into indiscriminate persecution of everyone, even "witch-hunt"-style violent purges.
- And in this process, G3 may have long been awakened by the Mother, awaiting some critical mass for collective action.

## IX. Core Conflict and Narrative Suggestions

### Multi-Layered Structure of Conflict

1. **Species conflict:** Survival competition between humans and the Mother's species.
2. **Internal conflict:** Struggle and betrayal among human nations, capital, and individuals.
3. **Identity conflict:** Self-recognition of G2 girls and G3 individuals — am I human, or monster? Where do I belong?
4. **Source conflict:** Naturally born G2 vs. farm-born G2 — are they sisters or enemies?

### Possible Narrative Threads

- **Protagonist:** Could be a local female doctor/social worker from the South American or African coast, both a witness to the disaster and a potential G1 victim. She must make the most painful choice between protecting her people and stopping the species invasion.
- **G2 protagonist:** A naturally born G2 girl who, after discovering her identity, tries to find a way out while being courted or opposed by farm-born G2 (whose hatred of humans is stronger).
- **Trilogy structure:**
  - **Part 1:** Covert infiltration and discovery. Following health-department investigation of abnormal birth data as the main line, gradually exposing the Mother's reproduction chain. The ending reveals the existence of G2 farms.
  - **Part 2:** Commodification and resistance. The world discovers the value of G2 and fights madly for it; G2 farms expand massively; some G2 awaken, escape, even organize underground resistance networks. The natural new Mother begins to awaken.
  - **Part 3:** Species war / coexistence. The new Mother's birth and the human-built Mother run in parallel. The final ending is not a simple "human victory" — perhaps a new world order, with humans and Mother's descendants coexisting in some way, but at what cost?

### Ultimate Ethical Questions

This story will force readers to face a series of questions with no standard answers:

- **If one day, an ancient intelligent species on the brink of extinction, due to environmental collapse, chooses to continue its race by modifying human reproduction — is this invasion, or a tragic survival?**
- **When humans discover they can make huge profits by trafficking G2 girls, who is the real monster?**
- **A G2 girl born on a farm, never treated as human, does she have the right to use the Mother's power to take revenge on humans?**
- **In the end, is there a path of coexistence between humans and the Mother's descendants? Or is one side doomed to extinction?**

## X. The Split of Social Ideology

When the Mother phenomenon gradually surfaces into the global view from the hidden coastal poor areas, human society does not form a unified front of opposition. Instead, different ideological groups begin to "explain" the Mother phenomenon through their own discourse systems and integrate it into their own political agendas. These interpretations conflict with one another, and also cause severe internal splits.

### 1. Feminism: Utopia or Illusion?

> *"This is a perfect society. Men only transition when needed."*

**Core Logic:**
- In the Mother system, women (G2) are the core of reproduction and evolution; men (sex-changed G2 men) are tool-like existences that can be "manufactured" on demand.
- Gender is no longer determined at birth but can be controlled by the Mother or technology. Women can keep their female identity, transitioning some individuals to male only when reproduction is needed.
- Radicals see this as a fundamental subversion of patriarchy: men are no longer the dominant gender, but a resource that can be summoned and consumed.

**Actions and Positions:**
- Publicly support the Mother's "liberation" of human women, even actively sending women to the Mother's waters to "accept evolution."
- Oppose any attempt to "cure" G2 or destroy the Mother, calling it a patriarchal backlash.
- Internal split: some advocate using artificial trigger technology to build a pure-female society; others are more radical, hoping all human men are replaced by G2.

**Narrative Tension:**
- When they discover that the "perfect society" they champion is actually a species-replacement plan — and that human women will eventually be eliminated as well — how does their faith collapse?
- Some G2 women are protected as "sisters," but do these G2 women really identify with human feminism?
- Possible plot: a feminist leader discovers she herself is G2, or her daughter is forcibly transitioned to male.

### 2. Environmental Organizations: The Ethics of Symbiosis

> *"This is humanity's sin. We should coexist with fellow beings of Earth."*

**Core Logic:**
- The Mother's invasion is a natural retaliation for human destruction of the marine environment (climate change, El Niño, pollution).
- Humans have no right to destroy the Mother's offspring, because humans are the invaders. Coexistence with the Mother's species should be sought — just as humans ideally coexist with other creatures.
- They believe humans can avoid total conflict by negotiating, sacrificing some coastal regions, or even actively helping the Mother complete its terrestrial transition.

**Actions and Positions:**
- Protect G2 and G3 from human slaughter; establish "coexistence zones" or "reserves."
- Lobby the United Nations to recognize the Mother's species' right to exist, demanding they be granted the legal status of "intelligent non-human."
- Oppose "G2 farms" and hysterectomy, considering them human-on-another-species genocide-style exploitation.

**Narrative Tension:**
- Environmentalists underestimate the Mother's intelligence: the Mother may not accept "coexistence" at all; its goal is replacement.
- When G3 begin their collective slaughter of humans, on which side do the environmentalists stand?
- Possible plot: some environmentalists are actually G3 unconsciously influenced by the Mother, who think they are pursuing coexistence but are actually buying time and space for the Mother.

### 3. LGBTQ+ Community: Posthuman Evolution

> *"This is human evolution."*

**Core Logic:**
- Sex-change ability breaks the fixity of the gender binary, demonstrating the biological possibility of gender fluidity.
- The existence of the Mother's descendants proves that humans can transcend current physiological limits and move toward a posthuman future.
- They see the Mother phenomenon as a naturally occurring gender liberation, not an invasion. The irreversibility of sex change is reinterpreted by them as "the complete freedom to choose to become another gender."

**Actions and Positions:**
- Support the rights of G2 and G3, seeing them as the ultimate allies of gender diversity.
- Protest violence against G2, hysterectomy, forced sex-change performances, viewing them as oppression based on gender fear.
- Some actively seek artificial trigger of sex change, hoping to "evolve" into G2 men.

**Narrative Tension:**
- When the LGBTQ+ community discovers that the Mother's sex change is not "choice" but "being triggered," and that the purpose is reproduction (not identity), does the support waver?
- Some sex-changed G2 men may find shelter in LGBTQ+ communities, but their experience is completely different from that of human trans people — they have no "identification" process, only the forced deprivation of their original identity.
- Possible plot: a famous LGBTQ+ activist is actually G3, whose mission is to use this community to win legitimacy and sympathy for the Mother.

### How These Ideologies Interweave Into the Story

These three voices are not mere background noise; they become the key forces driving conflict in the story:

| Position | Attitude toward the Mother | Attitude toward human government | Attitude toward black market / farms |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Radical feminists | Support, embrace | Oppose (patriarchy) | Case by case (cooperate if useful) |
| Eco-symbiosis | Coexist, protect | Criticize (destroying nature) | Firmly opposed |
| LGBTQ+ support | Evolution, celebrate | Demand rights recognition | Oppose exploitation |

**Conflicts between them:**
- Environmental organizations may have bloody conflicts with governments (protecting G3 from slaughter).
- Feminists may cooperate with black-market merchants (both need G2 women), but for completely opposite purposes.
- LGBTQ+ community may diverge from feminists (one emphasizes the freedom of gender fluidity, the other emphasizes the superiority of female identity).
- Individuals may belong to multiple groups, facing the tearing of multiple loyalties.

**Cracks within them:**
- Each group has its radicals, moderates, and "moles" infiltrated by the Mother.
- The story can arrange a character who is initially an activist of some movement, and later discovers that he/she or a loved one is G2/G3, falling into the collapse and reconstruction of faith.
