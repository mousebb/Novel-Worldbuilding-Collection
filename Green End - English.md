# World Setting Document: 《Green End》

---

## I. Core Concept

**One-line summary:**
Plants have launched a genetic revenge against humanity across geological ages — first using "toxin" to manufacture a survival panic, forcing humans to swallow the "antidote," and the antidote is itself the Trojan horse that rewrites the genes of descendants.

**Core Paradox:**
- Eat natural food → ingest S-Factor → have cognition rewritten, attack one's own kind
- Don't eat natural food (synthetic starch) → no S-Factor in the body → identified as "alien" by all S-Factor carriers, suffer attack
- Inject the An-Yi Serum → gain short-term immunity → descendants will inevitably undergo plantization

**Humans have no safe option. Every choice is the entry to a trap.**

## II. S-Factor (Silent Toxin) Detailed

### 2.1 Essence
- An organic compound with **dual pheromone-toxin attributes**.
- Synthesized and released synchronously by all vascular plants through a byproduct pathway of photosynthesis.
- The synthetic gene has existed in plant genomes for **about 230 million years**, predating mammalian radiation.
- It is the **ultimate defense protocol** reserved by the plant kingdom against "intelligent disruptors."

### 2.2 Transmission Path (Food-Chain Axis)
S-Factor **does not transmit through air**. It strictly follows the food-chain path:

| Tier | Vector | S-Factor Concentration |
|------|------|-----------|
| First | Plants (all vascular plants) | Baseline (1×) |
| Second | Herbivorous insects, herbivores | Concentrated 3–5× |
| Third | Small carnivores | Concentrated 15–20× |
| Fourth | Large carnivores, humans | Concentrated 50–70× |

### 2.3 Mechanism (Dual Strike)

**Toxin Effect (physiological):**
- After ingestion, binds to receptors on the hypothalamus-pituitary-adrenal axis.
- Triggers chronic inflammatory response; immune system stays in low-grade activation.
- Long-term ingestion leads to precursors of neurodegenerative disease.

**Pheromone Effect (behavioral):**
- S-Factor binds to specific receptors in the olfactory bulb and amygdala.
- Recodes "human scent" from "neutral / harmless" to "predator / threat."
- Affected individuals develop **an indiscriminate attack impulse toward humans who do not carry S-Factor.**
- This effect is amplified up the food chain; the higher the concentration, the stronger the aggression.

**Key Feature:** S-Factor carriers **do not attack each other**, because each other's scent is marked as "same kind." Attacks are directed only at **"blank-signal" individuals who do not carry S-Factor.**

## III. Synthetic Starch: Division, Not Salvation

### 3.1 Technical Background
Industrial-scale production of synthetic starch (CO₂ + hydrogen + electricity → starch) was achieved in the early 2030s. Before the S-Factor outbreak, there were 17 large synthetic-starch factories worldwide.

### 3.2 Fatal Defects Exposed
- Synthetic starch **contains no S-Factor**, because the raw materials (CO₂, water, catalyst) are not biological in origin.
- People who eat synthetic starch long-term have **zero S-Factor concentration in their bodies.**
- In plants' biological-recognition system, **zero concentration = blank signal = unregistered = threat.**

### 3.3 Consequences
- The first batch of synthetic-starch eaters were besieged by all S-Factor-carrying creatures around them — first by infected pets and livestock, then wildlife, and finally other humans.
- "Synthetic-starch communities" collapsed one after another between 2038 and 2040. Survivors were forced into closed facilities.
- The **Dome City-States** were thus born — not in pursuit of cleanliness, but to escape being hunted.

### 3.4 Ironic Summary
> Not eating plants won't make you safer. It only makes you **the only person in the world without a "green pass."** Every S-Factor-carrying creature will recognize you and hunt you. You flee into the dome, and the dome becomes your cage. And when you drink the "antidote" to return to the world, you have already handed yourself and your descendants over to the plants' timetable.

## IV. The Four Factions (Divided by Food-Chain Position)

### Faction 1: The Dome City-States

- **Population:** ~150 million
- **Core Bases:** Northern European underground dome clusters, deep facilities in the North American Shield, Antarctic deep-sea bases, Siberian permafrost underground cities.
- **Way of Life:**
  - Completely detached from the natural food chain.
  - Staple food: synthetic starch + closed-system hydroponic vegetables (sterilized, S-Factor-free).
  - All living spaces are airtight, sealed environments, physically isolated from the outside.
- **Technical Features:**
  - Air and water filtered and recycled through multiple layers.
  - Outside trips require full sealed hazard suits (to prevent contact with S-Factor carriers).
  - Gene-editing technology used to maintain sterile crop states.
- **Ideology:** **"Humans stay pure, refusing to be rewritten."**
- **Treatment of PPS (Plantization):** Strict isolation. At first sign, the patient is sent to an "Intervention Center" and forced into reversal with anti-lignin drugs. Success rate is extremely low, and the patient suffers greatly.
- **Internal Fissures:**
  - **Cleansers (mainstream):** View PPS as a new virus variant, advocate eradication.
  - **Truth Faction (underground):** A few scientists have begun to suspect the true nature of An-Yi Serum and are investigating in secret.
- **External Relations:**
  - Toward the Wasteland Hunters: **Hunt.** View them as "unvaccinated pathogen carriers," periodically organizing "biosecurity sweeps."
  - Toward the Root-Wood Forests: **Hostile.** Tree-person forest zones are listed as "red exclusion zones," monitored by drones.
  - Toward the Gray Market: **Depend but deny.** Secretly acquire scarce natural medicines through the Gray Market.

### Faction 2: The Root-Wood Forests

- **Population:** ~60 million in mid-transition; ~130 million fully converted (covering ~8.5 million km²).
- **Core Bases:** No fixed cities. Individuals are distributed across temperate and subtropical zones. Collective nodes are root-system junction zones (former Qinling, Appalachia, Black Forest, etc.).
- **Essence:** Mid-to-late-stage PPS individuals of the new generation, and the **collective consciousness network** of those who have fully transformed into tree-people.
- **Core Abilities:**
  - **Mycelial network communication:** Information transmission through underground fungal networks, with a coverage far exceeding human radio.
  - **Root erosion:** Slowly and irreversibly expanding forest boundaries, eroding ~7–12 mm of concrete structures per year.
  - **Consciousness resonance:** The consciousness of fully transformed individuals merges into the collective. Individual memories are preserved but take lower priority than overall directives.
- **Hidden Truth of Consciousness:** The individual consciousness of the tree-person **does not die — it is diluted.** The "I" still exists, but the voice becomes small. Fragments of individual memory occasionally surface when the wind passes through the canopy, forming weak signals that can be deciphered.
- **Ideology:** **"We do not attack. We simply grow. By the time the dome people walk out, only our scent will be left in the air."**
- **External Relations:**
  - Toward the Dome City-States: **Silent siege.** Planned planting of trees to gradually surround dome facilities.
  - Toward the Wasteland Hunters: **Cautious symbiosis.** Provides edible tree fruits, medicinal bark.
  - Toward the Gray Market: **Limited contact.** Not averse to barter exchange with the Gray Market.

### Faction 3: The Wasteland Hunters

- **Population:** ~110 million
- **Core Bases:** Remaining Amazon rainforest, Southeast Asian archipelagos, African inland plateaus, Pacific islands.
- **Way of Life:**
  - Remain in the natural food chain, but eat selectively.
  - Staple food: deep-layer fungi, high-altitude tubers, deep-water fish (S-Factor enrichment lower).
  - Avoid: herbivorous mammals, grains (high enrichment).
  - Through generations of selection, some groups have developed **mild tolerance** to S-Factor.
- **Ideology:** **Animism + fatalism.** Interpret S-Factor and PPS as "the Earth's way of replacing its children." Neither resist nor hate.
- **Key Role — "The Listeners":** A very few individuals who, by touching the earth with their feet, can sense the basic emotional fluctuations of the mycelial network. They are regarded as the clan's prophets.
- **External Relations:**
  - Toward the Dome City-States: **Hunted targets.** Survivors mostly live in remote areas outside dome reconnaissance range.
  - Toward the Root-Wood Forests: **Awed neighbors.** Multiple peaceful contacts have occurred.
  - Toward the Gray Market: **Main trading partner.** Exchange natural medicines for metal tools and antibiotics.

### Faction 4: The Gray Market Runners

- **Population:** ~20 million
- **Core Bases:** No fixed territory. Operate in the "gap zones" between the three factions — abandoned cities, underground tunnels, border outposts.
- **Essence:** Pragmatists with no stance. **No ideology — only trade.**
- **Core Business:**
  - Domes need rare natural medicines from the Wasteland.
  - Wasteland needs synthetic antibiotics from the Domes.
  - Root-Wood needs outside intelligence (trees cannot move).
  - The various sides need middlemen for information transfer.
- **Survival Skills:**
  - Fluent in the languages and etiquette of all sides.
  - Mastery of multiple secret routes.
  - Can coat their skin with camouflage coating that mimics S-Factor concentration (extracted from specific plants).
  - Every bite of food is a small self-poisoning. They trade lifespan for freedom, dosage for survival. They call it "occupational hazard."
- **Survival Philosophy:** **"Either side winning is fine, as long as I'm still alive when they win."**
- **External Relations:**
  - With all three factions: **transactional**, no alliance.
  - Internal unwritten rule: never reveal the trading details of one side to another.

## VII. PPS (Progressive Plantization Syndrome) Stages

| Stage | Age Range | Main Symptoms |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Latent** | 0–6 | No visible symptoms. Blood chlorophyll precursor levels ~47× higher than normal children. |
| **Initial (Greening)** | 7–10 | Chlorophyll-island deposits on either side of the spine, bright green. Increased thirst for water, decreased desire for cooked food. "Phototropism posture" — facing the window when falling asleep. |
| **Middle (Fibrosis)** | 11–14 | Skin surface lignifies, texture like dry bark. Internal organs begin to fibrose, partial functions replaced by chloroplast metabolism. Blood chlorophyll concentration reaches detectable levels. |
| **Late (Rooting)** | 15–18 | Lower-limb bone density increases by 230%; tailbone extends into a primary root cone. Strong affinity for soil; barefoot contact with soil produces deep-meditation brainwaves. |
| **Terminal (Transformation)** | 18–20 | Patients actively bury themselves in soil, depth ~1.2–1.5 m. Within 7–15 days, a tree (species varies by individual) grows from the burial site. |

## VIII. Core Contradictions and Themes

### 8.1 The Triple Trap
1. **Eat natural food** → become a monster attacking your own kind
2. **Eat synthetic food** → become an alien attacked by the whole world
3. **Inject An-Yi Serum** → save the present, bury the future

**Humans have no third path. Every path is the entry to a trap.**

### 8.2 Two Questions
- **"Are plants evolving, or are humans devolving?"** → The real answer: **plants use humanity's "evolution" (technology) to let humanity complete its own "evolution" (species replacement).**
- **"Who is the victim, who is the perpetrator?"** → What humans have done to plants (logging, burning, genetic modification) has been returned in kind by plants on a geological-time scale.

### 8.3 The Tragedy of Love
- The parents of the golden-needle generation had An-Yi Serum injected to protect their families.
- Now their children are in isolation zones with greenish skin, being forcibly "treated."
- A mother faces this choice: **let the doctor pull out her child's newly grown rootlets (physical pain), or let go of the child's hand and let them walk into the earth (psychic collapse).**

## X. Endgame Suspense (Optional Ending Directions)

1. **Split within the Root-Wood:** The collective consciousness experiences its first "self-doubt" — if symbionts (those who stopped transformation mid-way) can also live happily, is the full collectivization too thorough? A tree begins to think: "Am I wrong?"

2. **Crack inside the Dome:** A Dome's underground hydroponic supply pipe is pierced through the inner wall by Root-Wood tree roots. Trace S-Factor enters the vegetable system with the water. The maintenance worker finds a green biofilm on the pipe wall, but the repair queue is three months long — shutdown means ration cuts for the entire floor, performance demerits. He blasts the visible green away with a high-pressure hose, signs off "equipment normal." Three weeks later, residents who ate this batch of vegetables begin to exhibit aggressive behavior. The Dome is breached by **bureaucracy.**

3. **The Wasteland's Third Path:** The Listeners gather. The mycelia beneath their feet carry Root-Wood's harmony and the Dome's electromagnetic noise. An elderly Listener says: "**The Earth is asking us a question — which side will you choose? Or… will you choose a third side?**"

4. **The Longest Conversation:** The last dome-human stands on concrete ruins cracked by tree roots. Wind passes through the canopy, producing a low moan. He recognizes the melody — it is a lullaby his mother used to hum to him in childhood. **The tree remembers. The tree has always remembered.** This is not the end of revenge — it is the beginning of a conversation.

## XI. One-Line Summary

> **Plants do not allow humans to starve. Plants let humans choose for themselves — eat, and become their follower; don't eat, and become the enemy of the whole world; and when you swallow that "antidote" to have it both ways, you've already signed the land-transfer deed for your descendants.**
