## 《Human Material》Core Setting

---

### Background

In the near future, humanity achieves breakthroughs in brain transplantation and mechanical neuron-interface technology. After mechanical prosthetics become widespread, a new generation of hippies discovers that they can send stimulation signals to the brain through neural interfaces, causing it to over-secrete dopamine and creating a cheap "electronic drug." The medical community further discovers that excessive dopamine secretion is accompanied by a longevity substance called **"Taciturnin."** This discovery is exploited by drug cartels, giving birth to the **Human Material** industry — using live human brains as raw material, alternating extreme pleasure and pain to continuously extract Taciturnin, which is then sold to the wealthy in pursuit of immortality. As a result, a vast underground plantation network emerges across the global North and South, where transnational capital and criminal syndicates become deeply intertwined, and the industry is gradually legalized.

### Main Characters

- **Lin Yuan**: A former neurosurgeon. After his younger sister is killed, he takes the alias "The Mourner" and becomes the leader of "The Faders," a group that carries out bloody revenge against the plantations using mechanical prosthetics.
- **Su Mingzhe**: Lin Yuan's mentor, discoverer of Taciturnin, who ultimately chooses to merge with the power behind the industry.
- **Qiao Wei**: Su Mingzhe's assistant. She perfects the separation and purification of Taciturnin and eventually becomes the Chief Technology Officer of the "Holy Blood" Group.
- **Xiao He (Little Lotus)**: Lin Yuan's sister, an innocent devoured by the Human Material industry — the spiritual symbol of the entire work.

### Story Outline

#### Chapter 1 · The Faders

Lin Yuan meets financier Zhao Yongchang in a Tokyo clinic. Using the fact that the financier's own daughter has become raw material for the Human Material industry as his opening, he confronts him over how his investments fueled the global plantation system. Through the confrontation between the two men, the origin and operational logic of the Human Material industry are revealed. The fate of Lin Yuan's sister Xiao He is disclosed for the first time as a hidden thread.

#### Chapter 2 · The Reapers

Told primarily from Qiao Wei's perspective, this chapter descends into "The Garden of Eternal Life," an underground plantation in the Amazon, and coldly depicts the industrial-scale production of Human Material. Lin Yuan leads The Faders in a raid on the base, using a neural disruptor to hijack Qiao Wei's prosthetic body and turn it against her. The plantation is destroyed, but Lin Yuan knows this is merely the tip of the iceberg.

#### Chapter 3 · The Mourner

Five years later, Lin Yuan intercepts "Project Angel" in Jakarta — an industrial-scale upgrade in which gene-edited babies are directly cultivated as Human Material. During the operation, he is cornered by the UN's "Wrath of Heaven" task force, and the truth is revealed: his mentor Su Mingzhe is the mastermind behind the operation. Lin Yuan's mechanical body is stripped away, and his brain is reduced to Human Material inside a plantation. In a false dream of bliss, he receives a coded signal from his comrades, refuses to succumb, and prepares to launch a final counterattack from the very heart of enemy territory.

### Core Themes

> When the human body becomes medicine and emotion becomes a mineral deposit, does a civilization that has utterly instrumentalized life still deserve "immortality"?

The story, through Lin Yuan's inversion from avenger to Human Material, interrogates the boundaries of technological ethics, capital logic, and human nature.

### Character Profiles

#### Lin Yuan

- **Identity:** Former neurosurgeon, later leader of The Faders, codename "The Mourner."
- **Background:** Su Mingzhe's student. After his sister Xiao He is killed by the Human Material industry, he implants his brain into a custom combat prosthetic and walks the path of revenge.
- **Personality:** Outwardly cold and rational, inwardly driven by hatred — yet with lingering warmth for his sister and an undying moral compass.
- **State in Chapter 3:** Stripped of his mechanical body, his brain becomes Human Material. In a false dream, he awaits his counterattack.

#### Su Mingzhe

- **Identity:** Neuroscience professor, Lin Yuan's mentor, Qiao Wei's academic guide.
- **Contribution:** In 2080, he publishes a paper that first names and describes Taciturnin, laying the theoretical foundation of the Human Material industry.
- **Key Turning Point:** After the paper's publication, he chooses to cooperate with the "Guardians of Civilization" and becomes one of the behind-the-scenes architects of the industry.
- **Relationship with Lin Yuan:** Viewed by Lin Yuan as a spiritual beacon. The revelation of his betrayal in Chapter 3 is the work's greatest emotional wound.

#### Qiao Wei

- **Identity:** PhD in neurobiology, Su Mingzhe's assistant, later CTO of the "Holy Blood" Group.
- **Contribution & Role:** Completes the technical translation of Taciturnin separation and purification, designs 3,700 emotional-stimulation "menu packages" — the key technical executor who turns the Human Material industry from theory to mass production.
- **Fate:** In Chapter 2, killed by Lin Yuan with a neural disruptor that hijacks her prosthetic body, dying in the ruins of the Garden of Eternal Life.

#### Zhao Yongchang

- **Identity:** Manager of the largest bio-risk investment fund in all of Asia; Amy's father.
- **Role:** Confrontation target in Chapter 1, representing the elite class that has indirectly fueled the Human Material industry without realizing it. His investments flow into "New Hope Medical Technologies," while his own daughter Amy becomes Human Material — closing a circle of fate in bitter irony.

#### Xiao He (Little Lotus)

- **Identity:** Lin Yuan's sister.
- **Symbolism throughout the work:** She never officially appears, existing only in Lin Yuan's memory, in a holographic frame, and in near-death hallucinations. She is the original driver of all his actions, representing the devoured innocence of this world.

#### Phantom (幽灵)

- **Identity:** Deputy commander of The Faders, fully digitized consciousness, free to roam the network.
- **Function:** Intelligence core. Leaves a key code at the end of Chapter 3 to pass the secret phrase "await the signal" to Lin Yuan, hinting at the possibility of a counterattack.
