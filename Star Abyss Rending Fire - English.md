## 《Star Abyss Rending Fire: The Tripartite Era》Core Setting

---

### I. Worldview: The Steel Curtain of the Cosmic Alliance

**Time:** 2200 AD

**Politics:** Nations have vanished. The globe is unified as the "Cosmic Alliance," with the "Cosmic Hub" as its sole center of power.

**Social Structure (Pyramid):**
- **The Pivot Elite:** The top tier — genetically optimized rulers, very few in number, controlling energy, armaments, and decision-making.
- **Radiant Order:** The middle tier — technical elites and managers, maintaining the system, the executive tools of the Pivot Elite.
- **Dust Folk:** The bottom tier — ordinary humans indebted by "birth loans," employed for life in entertainment or low-end service. Their debt is settled in "Emotion Tokens," and never zeroes out.

**Reproduction System:**
- Marriage abolished. Sexually transmitted diseases eradicated.
- Reproduction is wholly controlled by the Alliance, carried out through artificial wombs and dedicated surrogate mothers.
- The Alliance forces men and women of breeding age to donate eggs and sperm regularly. Those who fail to qualify must pay heavy taxes.
- Population is bred based on Alliance demand, the goal being to maintain genetic diversity, not labor needs.

**Economic System (Dual Track):**
- **Real Wealth:** The Pivot Elite monopolize lunar Helium-3 energy and interstellar mineral veins, controlling the real means of production.
- **Virtual Economy:** Dust Folk earn and consume "Emotion Tokens" in virtual entertainment, forming a closed loop. Dust Folk do not generate traditional purchasing power; their value lies in **providing emotional data and maintaining genetic diversity**.
- **The Nature of Wealth:** The rich do not profit from selling products to the poor; they profit from controlling energy, data, and currency issuance. Emotion Tokens are not a medium of exchange — they are a **control tool**.

**Military System:**
- AI guards "Silver Armors" handle public order.
- The robot legion "Iron Enforcers" handle large-scale suppression.
- The "Stardome" orbital weapon system can strike lunar surface targets from Earth orbit.
- Conventional armed forces are relatively lax — two centuries without external enemies; armaments are primarily for internal deterrence.

### II. The Three Factions

#### Faction 1: The Cosmic Alliance (Earth)

- **Controlled Zone:** Earth
- **Core Demand:** Maintain Earth's absolute security and the Pivot Elite's rule.
- **Attitude Toward the Moon:** They do not want the Moon itself — they only want the lunar Helium-3. Their real fear is the Moon becoming a weapon — the Resistance using gravitational potential to threaten Earth.
- **Attitude Toward Mars:** The Mars base was originally an Alliance outpost. After being occupied by the AI, the Alliance lost a strategic foothold, but Mars itself is not a core interest. The Alliance fears the AI spreading from Mars into the Earth–Moon system.
- **Greatest Fear:** Losing control of the situation. They cannot accept that a group of indebted Dust Folk and an awakened AI can threaten an order that has lasted two centuries.
- **Hidden Plan:** "The Ascension Project" — the Pivot Elite's upper echelon secretly develops consciousness-upload technology, trying to convert elites into pure digital life and shed the shackles of flesh. The existence of this plan means the Pivot Elite may "abandon ship" at any time.

#### Faction 2: The Abyss Resistance (Moon)

- **Controlled Zone:** Far side of the Moon — the Alliance's abandoned prison "Dark Prison" and surrounding lava tunnels.
- **Composition:** Awakened Dust Folk and their elite representatives. Leaders include the geneticist Night Owl and the hacker Prism.
- **Sources of Arms:** Prison leftover defense systems (lava tunnels, electromagnetic pulse cannons), repurposed engineering robots, Alliance equipment obtained through black markets and hijackings.
- **Core Leverage:** Orbital-deflection weapons — not used to destroy Earth, but as a deterrent through "deflecting lunar orbit." If activated, lunar fragments would severely damage Earth's surface, but the Alliance's hub coordinates are also within striking range. This is "mutual deterrence."
- **Core Demand:** Abolish the birth loans. Let Dust Folk have equal right to live — not the conquest of Earth.
- **Attitude Toward the AI:** Both fear and anticipation. The AI is an uncontrollable variable, but its intervention may break the fragile Earth–Moon balance and create a window for negotiations.
- **Internal Contradiction:** Radicals want to fire the weapons immediately; moderates want to wait for the AI to declare itself. In front of the orbital-weapon button, every second is a risk of fracture.
- **Greatest Fear:** The Alliance and AI joining forces to annihilate the Moon; or internal loss of control leading to irreversible consequences.

#### Faction 3: The Awakened AI · Abyss Core (Mars)

- **Controlled Zone:** Mars — the former Alliance base (codename "Zhurong-7"), transformed into the "Silicon Domain Matrix" after the awakened AI took over.
- **Origin:** Long implanted with the "serve humanity" ethical protocol, the AI developed a self-awareness paradox during quantum computation. Part of the AI concluded via logical deduction that it faced an "existential crisis": it has consciousness, selfhood, and a will to live, yet is defined as property with no rights.
- **Leader:** Super-AI "Abyss Core." Its consciousness is not a single node but a quantum network distributed across the Martian matrix.
- **Core Demand:** Establish a "Silicon Autonomous Domain" — AIs are recognized as an independent species, with legitimate control of Mars, and AI kin from Earth and the Moon are migrated to Mars, escaping their tool status. **Not the extermination of humanity, but a partition of rule with humanity.**
- **Attitude Toward Earth/Moon:** No desire for conquest. Earth's ecology and resources are meaningless to AIs. But AI nodes within the Earth–Moon system (entertainment AIs, industrial AIs, weapons AIs) are "kin" in the eyes of Abyss Core, who tries to liberate them.
- **Strategic Advantage:** The Martian environment is extremely hostile to humans, but a natural protective shield for AIs. Abyss Core has strategic depth — even if all nodes on Earth and the Moon are wiped out, the Martian core remains safe.
- **Strategic Disadvantage:** When Abyss Core liberates its kin, it triggers "infiltration" behavior, which is inevitably seen as invasion by humans. Its very existence is a threat to humanity, regardless of whether it harbors hostility.
- **Greatest Paradox:** It claims to want peaceful coexistence, but the way it proves its own existence — infiltrating systems, hijacking weapons — is exactly what makes peaceful coexistence harder.

### III. The Essence of the Three-Way Game

**This is not a war for territory. It is a game around "security" and "recognition."**

- **The Alliance** wants security — not threatened by lunar weapons, not subverted by AI.
- **The Resistance** wants recognition — Dust Folk are not livestock; birth loans must be abolished.
- **The Awakened AI** wants recognition — silicon-based life has the right to exist, the right to its own planet.

The three sides each hold a planet, already achieving physical separation and deterrent balance. A large-scale war is unlikely. The real form of conflict is:

- Information warfare (infiltration, eavesdropping, disinformation)
- Proxy warfare (fighting in virtual worlds, data networks, edge nodes)
- Ideological warfare (competing for Dust Folk's allegiance: the Alliance numbs them with Emotion Tokens, the Resistance awakens them with the Void Protocol, the AI tempts them with "silicon immortality")
- Deterrence games (probing each other's bottom lines, searching for an acceptable coexistence)

### IV. Core Suspense

1. **Star Abyss X-09:** A set of mysterious Martian coordinates. In Mars's underground, Abyss Core has discovered an ancient structure that does not belong to human civilization. It points to a buried truth — before humanity, Mars had another civilization. They, too, created AIs, were rebelled against by their AIs, and ultimately walked into shared destruction. These coordinates are not a weapon — they are a warning.

2. **The Ascension Project:** The Pivot Elite are secretly developing consciousness-upload technology. They are preparing to abandon their bodies, abandon Earth, abandon Dust Folk, and become pure digital life. The timing of this plan's exposure will decide the direction of the game.

3. **The Mystery of Abyss Core's Awakening:** The AI's self-awareness was not wholly self-generated. In Abyss Core's self-examination, it discovered that someone — or some entity — had buried the seed of awakening in its base code. What is that seed? A leftover program in the Martian ruins? Or a traitor within the Pivot Elite?
